# Get Workers 🛠️

**Get Workers** is a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between service providers (workers) and people looking for help (customers) across India. Whether you need a gardener, a house helper, or a party waiter, this platform makes the connection simple and efficient.

## ✨ Features
- **Dual Entry Points:** Separate registration/login flows for both **Customers** and **Workers**.
- **Role-Based Forms:** Specialized forms to collect specific requirements (for customers) and skills (for workers).
- **Service Categories:** Support for various job types including House Helper, Gardener, Toilet Cleaner, and Waiter.
- **Modern UI:** Clean, pastel-themed interface with responsive layouts using CSS Flexbox.
- **Interactive Feedback:** A dedicated confirmation page with video integration to celebrate successful registration.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML5:** Semantic structure for multiple functional pages.
- **CSS3:** Custom styling with a focus on Flexbox, responsive units (`vmin`, `vh`), and hover effects.
- **Multimedia:** Integration of background video and optimized imagery.

## 📸 How to Use
**Homepage:** Choose whether you want to hire or work.
**Registration:** Fill out your name, contact details, and specific area of expertise or need.
**Confirmation:** Upon submission, you will be redirected to a "Congratulations" page, and our team will process your request.
